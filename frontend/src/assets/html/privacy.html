Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how meetjeffolsen.com (the "Site") handles personal data in connection with the portfolio website and its demonstration Content Management System (CMS).

1. Data We Collect

We collect only your email address when you register for or log in to the CMS demonstration. No other personal information is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your email address is used solely to send One-Time Passwords (OTPs) for authentication. It is not used for marketing, newsletters, or any other purpose, and is never sold or shared with third parties.

OTP emails are delivered via Resend, a third-party transactional email service. Resend receives your email address only for the purpose of sending the requested authentication email.

3. Data Retention and Automatic Deletion

All CMS accounts, associated profile data, and email addresses are automatically and permanently deleted 7 days after account creation, regardless of activity. You may also delete your account at any time from your profile dashboard. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

4. IP Addresses

Your IP address is processed transiently by the server for rate limiting purposes. It is not logged, stored, or associated with your account.

5. Children's Privacy

The CMS demonstration is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the CMS constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
